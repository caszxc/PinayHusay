Janelle Mae Frayna, born on May 19, 1996, is a distinguished Filipino chess player celebrated for her remarkable achievements on the international stage. In 2016, she made history by becoming the Philippines' first Woman Grandmaster (WGM) during the Chess Olympiad held in Baku, Azerbaijan.
